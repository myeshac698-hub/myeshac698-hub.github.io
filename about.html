About

Hi, I'm Myesha.

I started this blog as a place to keep track of my thoughts on the things I read, watch, listen to, and occasionally become obsessed with. Most of these posts are less like formal reviews and more like conversations with myself that I decided to write down.

You'll find thoughts on books, movies, television, music, and whatever else happens to capture my attention. I also post weekly roundups to document what I've been enjoying lately.

A Few Things I Like

  • Books that leave me thinking for days afterward
  • Movies that I will talk about for at least a whole week
  • Video games that make me so hooked in I don't even realize the sun is rising
  • TV shows that make me want to pull an all nighter to binge the whole season
  • Songs that I can lie on the floor and let the sounds wash over me
  • Figure skating
  • Museums, art, and creative projects
  • Trying new foods and recipes
  • Nature
  • Fashion, makeup, and nails

Why This Blog Exists

Part archive, part journal, part recommendation list.

I wanted a place where I could collect my thoughts in one spot instead of scattering them across different apps and websites. This blog is my attempt to build a small corner of the internet that's entirely my own.
